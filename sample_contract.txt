VENDOR SERVICES AGREEMENT — ABC Vendor

1. TERM. This Agreement begins on January 1, 2026 and continues for twelve (12) months.

2. PAYMENT TERMS. The Client shall pay the Vendor a monthly fee of $5,000, due within
thirty (30) days of each invoice. Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month.

3. TERMINATION. Either party may terminate this Agreement with sixty (60) days written
notice. The Client may terminate immediately for material breach if not cured within
fifteen (15) days of notice.

4. NON-COMPETE. During the term and for twelve (12) months after termination, the Vendor
shall not provide similar services to any direct competitor of the Client.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY. Both parties agree to keep all proprietary information confidential
for a period of three (3) years following termination.
